Basic Steps to Calculate VAT

To calculate VAT, you only need a simple calculator and a few steps. First, enter the price of the item and then apply the VAT percentage. This is the easiest method for beginners learning how to add vat on a calculator.

Multiply the price by 23 and divide by 100 to get VAT. Then add this amount to the original price to get the final total. This method is widely used in Ireland for quick calculations.

Another shortcut is multiplying the price by 1.23. This gives the total amount directly without extra steps. It is fast and commonly used in business invoices.

Food items, exports, and books may have lower or zero VAT. Services like hotels and tourism may use reduced rates. Knowing this helps avoid confusion in pricing.
